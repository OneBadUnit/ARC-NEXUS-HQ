Photo Gather

Free Windows Tool

What it does

Photo Gather helps you locate pictures, then copies them into a new folder organized by year, month, and day.

It also creates a simple CSV file — best opened in Excel or a similar program — so you can see where each copied photo originally came from.

Your original pictures are never deleted or changed.

What you download

Photo Gather is a single file you open on Windows. Nothing gets installed on your computer.

If you do not want it anymore, just delete the file.

What it can access

By default, Photo Gather only looks in normal user folders, such as Pictures.

It will not scan your whole computer unless you specifically choose that location.

Quick safety note

Be careful with any file you download from the internet. Only download from sources you trust, keep antivirus protection active, and do not run anything that feels suspicious.
